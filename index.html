I am a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Business and Economics, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam, where I also teach courses at the John Stuart Mill College and Amsterdam University College. Previously, I was a PPE PhD candidate, under the supervision of René van den Brink and Martin van Hees, and a Junior lecturer at the John Stuart Mill College, Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. My research mainly lies in, and at the intersection of, philosophy and economics using tools from game theory, social networks, and social choice theory. My thesis was on measuring voting power in hierarchical organisations.
